Our Services

Fastforty imports and exports cars,automotives and medical equipments across the world

A customer is furnished with below documents once he/she engages with us

  • Import documents
  • Auction sheet
  • Export certificate/original logbook
  • Translated logbook
  • Inspection certificate by QISJ
  • Bill of Lading- shippingline

Products

Source customer's products based on factors such mileage, model, colour, healthy standards, price. Customers' preference supersedes our opinion on choice of car to purchase

Radiation test and Car inspections

Radiation test to ensure cars dont possess harmful radiactive elemments.Cars inspected before being purchased to ensure they are in perfect condition and have functioning parts

Car purchases

Purchased from auction houses, indivduals, manufactures, companies and provide customer with auction sheets

  • Cash in hand-Minimum 70% of car's price required.
  • We accept bank loans

Shipping

We ferry all products sourced through us by ship. Once all export documents have been signed at origin country's port, products are loaded into the ship and moved to their destined countries.We provide only two ways of shipment mainly Roro and containers.Import exercise will take at most 3 months once all details pertaining the trade have been finalised

Consultation services

We guide customers on the best models to purchase based on their geographical regions. Also, we offer legal assistance to our clients in cases involving our company's products
