Frequently Asked Questions

What does Fastforty importers and Exporters company deal in?

Fastforty company imports cars, plant automotives and machinery to Kenya and also sells to other countries. Recently, we have also import medical equipements and we have standby medical personnel to advise on type and quality

How do you source for your car?

From the auction,Individuals,Manufacturers and Companies

How do you ensure cars being imported are of good quality and meets health standards?

All cars must undergo inspections by KEBS appointed bodies like OISJ. All cars imported through us must undergo radiation tests before shipping and upon arrival to destination. we dont buy cars from auction houses around areas which were affected by nuclear bombs some years back like Hiroshima and Nagasaki

Which type of cars do you import?

We sell all car models from petrol, diesel and electric powered

Do you import heavy machinery like tractors, forklifts?

We at Fastforty, we source for all types of items and machinery

How do you do shipping?

Roro,Container.40ft and 20ft

How sure that my goods will arrive safely?

All our shipping services are insurance based making us more safe for all our client. Once cars reach the port, they are done comprehensive insurance to mitigate against any loss

Do you keep clients' history?

We keep duplicate copies of all slients' records and we have a database which we have stored the records

Generally, the process can take 1 to 4 months. It will depend with client's budget
